Is your clinic truly data compliant in the age of AI?

Answer 3 quick questions below to find out.

1. Can you produce your TOMS (Technical & Organisational Measures) within 24 hours if requested?

As required under UAE & EU data protection regulations for demonstrating accountability.

2. Can you guarantee your team are not using public LLMs or apps that store patient data outside the UAE?

Data residency requirements under PDPL & ADHICS prohibit unverified AI or recording apps.

3. Do your current policies, training, and consent forms reflect today’s technological landscape?

Strong governance is key to mitigating compliance risks as technology evolves.

Breaches can result in serious penalties. You cannot delegate your liability — not knowing is not a defence.
